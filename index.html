About us

We are Th么 and Cong, a vietnamese couple who lives in a small village in northern Vietnam. We love to share our daily life with people from all over the world, that's why we've created a place where we can show our lifestyle and enjoy each others company. We don't want Tay culture to get lost, so we hope it can be preserved if more people come and experience it by themselves.

The Homestay

We are located in Na Rang Village, surrounded by mountains and nature. In here you can find the traditional Tay home where we share our space to sleep with individual privacy. Also we have two shared bathrooms and a hot water shower you can use when you want. There is a big common area with fresh tea, hammocks and a garden to enjoy. We will cook and share breakfast, lunch and dinner provided by our own garden or local ingredients. Furthermore, we have some livestock, fruit trees and lots of walks around the area where you can get lost.

The Village

Na Rang is a tiny village surrounded by other little towns inside of the mountains in the province of Ha Giang. This place is unique since it preserves Tay culture and lifestyle and is looking forward to share it so it won't get lost. Here you can find old ways of rice harvesting, Tay festivities, traditions and many more! You can easily get in here by bus from Ha Giang, ask us! Find us here
